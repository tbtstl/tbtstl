Karpathy's autoresearch inspired kv. I wanted to dump internet sources into a system that linked their ideas. I also built a Chrome extension that saves articles to its inbox.

kv stores its vault as Markdown. Each heartbeat turns saved sources into linked source and concept notes, then finds adjacent sources or writes a memo. It is currently very schizophrenic, and I think I like that.
